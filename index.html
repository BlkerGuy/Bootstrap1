Yamaha YZF-R6

Yamaha YZF-R6 is a middleweight supersport motorcycle from the Japanese manufacturer. It has a razor-sharp aggressive design and a powerful engine. It has a forward-inclined engine with thrilling performance. This is one of the most popular supersport bikes in its class, with premium features and high-end components.
